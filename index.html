PROJECTS

LOGO DESIGNS

A logo is the "face" of a brand—a visual shorthand
that tells the world who you are and what you stand
for without saying a word.

FLYER DESIGNS

A flyer act as the "messenger." It is a single-page advertisement—digital
or printed—design created to grab attention quickly and drive a specific action,
like attending an event or buying a product.

PROMO/SALES DESIGNS

A promo and sales design is the high-stakes side of graphic design.
Unlike a general brand flyer, its sole purpose is to convert a viewer into a customer—fast.
It’s a psychological game of creating urgency and value through visuals.

BUSINESS CARD DESIGNS

A business card is the physical (or digital) extension of a handshake.
It is a compact, powerful branding tool designed to provide essential contact information
while leaving a lasting professional impression.

STICKER DESIGNS

A sticker serves two main purposes: functional labeling
(ingredient lists, prices) or brand expression (logo "swag" and decorative art).

BANNER DESIGNS

A banner is a large-format display designed for high visibility
from a distance. The banner’s job is to command attention and anchor a brand's space.

BRAND IDENTITY DESIGNS

A brand identity design is the entire "personality" and
visual system that represent a brand. It is the deliberate process of creating a
cohesive look and feel that ensures a business is recognized across every touchpoint.

CELEBRATION DESIGNS

A celebration designs are all about emotion, energy, and atmosphere.
Whether it’s for a birthday, a wedding, an anniversary, or a religious holiday,
these designs are meant to feel festive and inviting rather than just functional.
